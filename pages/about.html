About Us...

Welcome to Camden Vintage, the home of hand sourced vintage pieces, based in the heart of Camden town. We are a small, family run business who started in Camden market almost 15 years ago. Over this time we have built up a strong connection with the community, gained experience in sourcing unique pieces and collaborated with some of the leading stylists for film, TV and music. You can see our items in productions from the BBC, ITV, Netflix and many more! We are open every day of the week and welcome all to visit our store. We look forward to seeing you!

- The Camden Vintage team
