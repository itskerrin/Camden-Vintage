Our products

Here at Camden Vintage we have a range of hand sourced vintage clothing, accessories and shoes. All of our items are hand picked by us from our favourite suppliers in Italy, the U.S and the U.K. Some of our stock includes; Burberry raincoats, Barbour jackets, Rayban sunglasses, Doc Martens and many more. We also supply costume clothing and vintage army uniforms which are often used in stage and film productions.
