Notera 2 is a handwritten typeface drawn and created by Måns Grebäck in 2018. 
It is a recreation of the popular Notera font, extended to five weights: 
Thin, Light, Medium, Bold and Black. 

With its hundreds of glyphs, Notera 2 supports a very wide range of languages. 
The font contains OpenType features such as stylistic and contextual alternate alphabets for a genuine handwritten style. 

Together with the regular font, also included is the Notera 2 Underline font, for decorative underlining.
